Understand first.  Decide second.
Do I need crypto experience?
No. We handle wallets, transactions, and gas fees. You deposit money and withdraw money.
Is this a bank?
No. Your money goes into DeFi lending protocols, not a bank account. Different product, different risk. That's why we're fully transparent.
What are the risks?
This is DeFi, not a guaranteed deposit. Smart contract bugs, protocol failures, or stablecoin depegs could cause losses. Only deposit what you can afford to lose.
Is this like Luna/UST?
No. We use USDC, backed 1:1 by real dollars and audited monthly. Luna's UST was algorithmic with no backing. That's why it collapsed.
What currency are my savings in?
Your money is converted to USDC, a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. This means your balance is in dollars. If the dollar weakens against your local currency, your returns may be lower when you withdraw.
How much can I earn?
4-7% APY, depending on market demand. Rates fluctuate. When more people want to borrow, rates go up.
Is my money locked?
No. Withdraw anytime, no fees, no lockups.
How can I verify everything?
Every transaction is on-chain. Check your funds anytime with your wallet address.
