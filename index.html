Understand first. Decide second.
Do I need crypto experience?
No. You deposit money, watch it grow, and withdraw. We handle everything behind the scenes.
Is this a bank?
No. Banks pay you almost nothing because they use your money their way. Here, your money earns more but it's not government-insured. That's the tradeoff.
What are the risks?
The system could have a bug. The stablecoin could lose value. These are unlikely but possible. Never deposit money you can't afford to lose.
Is this like Luna/UST?
No. Luna collapsed because it wasn't backed by real money. USDC is—every dollar is held in reserve and audited monthly.
What currency are my savings in?
Your money is converted to USDC, a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. This means your balance is in dollars. If the dollar weakens against your local currency, your returns may be lower when you withdraw.
How much can I earn?
4-7% per year. Rates change based on demand,some weeks higher, some lower. You'll always see your current rate in the app.
Is my money locked?
No. Withdraw anytime, no fees, no lockups.
How can I verify everything?
You get a public link to your account. Click it anytime to see every deposit, earning, and withdrawal, recorded permanently.
